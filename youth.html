Youth Ministry

Growing Young People in Christ and Mission

Youth camp, mini camps, prayer, Bible study, leadership, evangelism, and service for the youth of SDA Church Kofiase District.

Our Focus

Faith That Becomes Service

The Youth Ministry helps young people build a personal walk with Jesus, grow in Scripture, serve their communities, and use their gifts in worship and mission.

Through youth camp, mini camps, AY programs, Bible study, outreach, music, and fellowship, we create space for young people to pray together, ask honest questions, lead with humility, and become active disciples in the church family.

Camps & Gatherings

Key Youth Ministry Programs

These programs help young people grow spiritually, build healthy friendships, discover their gifts, and serve beyond the church walls.

C

Youth Camp

A major gathering for worship, Bible study, morning devotion, group discussions, leadership training, recreation, and mission-focused fellowship.

M

Mini Camps

Shorter local camp experiences for prayer, team building, practical lessons, music, games, and preparation for larger district youth programs.

R

Retreats

Focused prayer and Bible retreats that help youth pause, reflect, and renew their commitment to Christ.

A

AY Programs

Adventist Youth programs with Bible quizzes, discussions, testimonies, special music, drama, and practical Christian living topics.

L

Leadership Training

Training for young people who want to lead worship, organize programs, mentor others, and serve with responsibility.

F

Fellowship & Recreation

Healthy social activities, sports, talent moments, group games, and friendships that strengthen belonging in the church family.

Youth Activities

Ways Young People Can Get Involved

P

Prayer & Devotion

Regular prayer moments and devotionals that help youth grow in a real relationship with Christ.

B

Bible Study

Interactive study, Sabbath School participation, and practical conversations around Scripture.

S

Service Projects

Community outreach, health programs, visitation, clean-up exercises, and practical acts of compassion.

M

Music & Worship

Choir, special music, praise, and creative worship support for Sabbath, AY, camp, and district programs.

E

Evangelism

Youth-led witnessing, Bible interests, invitation, literature sharing, and mission-focused events.

T

Talent Development

Opportunities for young people to grow in speaking, singing, media, organizing, teaching, and practical ministry skills.
