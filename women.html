Women's Ministry

Women Growing in Faith, Care, and Service

Prayer, Bible study, mentorship, family support, fellowship, and compassionate outreach for women across the district.

Our Focus

Discipleship With A Caring Heart

The Women's Ministry encourages women to grow in Christ, support one another, strengthen families, and serve the church and community with compassion.

Through prayer circles, Bible study, mentoring, visitation, practical training, and outreach, women are equipped to minister with wisdom, courage, and love.

View Ministry Events

Programs

How Women's Ministry Serves

P

Prayer Circles

Small prayer groups that intercede for families, the church, youth, the sick, and community needs.

B

Bible Study

Scripture-based discussions that help women grow in faith, character, stewardship, and practical Christian living.

M

Mentorship

Older and experienced women encouraging younger women in faith, family, service, and life decisions.

F

Family Life Support

Teaching and fellowship around marriage, parenting, home worship, conflict care, and spiritual leadership at home.

O

Compassionate Outreach

Visitation, welfare support, care for vulnerable members, and practical help for families in need.

S

Skills & Stewardship

Practical sessions on health, home management, small business, financial stewardship, and service skills.

Get Involved

Ways Women Can Participate

V

Visitation Teams

Join teams that visit members, pray with families, and encourage those who are sick or absent.

T

Teaching & Sharing

Lead devotionals, facilitate discussions, share testimonies, or teach practical life and ministry skills.

C

Care Projects

Support food drives, welfare needs, baby-care support, widows' care, and community service projects.
