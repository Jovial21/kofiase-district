Our Story

Faithful Witness in Kofiase

SDA Church Kofiase District exists to nurture a Christ-centered Adventist community across Kofiase and nearby towns. Our members gather each week for worship, Bible study, prayer, service, and practical ministry to families.

From Sabbath School to youth evangelism, our work is shaped by Scripture, compassion, and the call to make disciples.

The Kofiase District comprises seven local churches serving communities across the district.
