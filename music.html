Overview

Worship Through Music

Our Music Ministry leads Christ-centered worship through choir, praise teams, and instrumental ensembles. We support congregational worship, special events, and music training for all ages.

Opportunities

Sing, Play, Serve

  • Choir rehearsals and performances
  • Praise team participation
  • Instrumental support and training
  • Children’s and youth music programs

All musical skill levels are welcome. Contact the Music Ministry coordinator to join rehearsals or volunteer.

Get Involved

Join a Team

Whether you sing, play, or support, there’s a place for you. We hold auditions and training as needed and welcome volunteers for concerts and outreach.
