Health Ministry

Whole-Person Health for Church and Community

Health education, wellness seminars, screenings, lifestyle teaching, and compassionate community care shaped by Adventist health principles.

Our Focus

Serving Body, Mind, and Spirit

The Health Ministry encourages members and the wider community to care for the body as God's gift while growing in balanced spiritual, mental, and physical wellbeing.

Through practical education, wellness programs, screenings, nutrition lessons, and community outreach, we help families make healthier daily choices.

Programs

How Health Ministry Serves

W

Wellness Seminars

Teaching on nutrition, rest, water, exercise, temperance, mental wellbeing, and healthy daily routines.

S

Health Screenings

Community screening days for blood pressure, weight, basic wellness checks, and health guidance.

N

Nutrition Education

Practical food demonstrations, healthy meal ideas, family nutrition tips, and simple lifestyle changes.

E

Exercise & Activity

Encouragement for walking clubs, fitness challenges, active living, and healthy recreation.

M

Mental Wellbeing

Supportive conversations around stress, grief, family pressure, emotional health, and hope in Christ.

O

Community Outreach

Health talks, local outreach, school or community programs, and compassionate care for practical needs.
