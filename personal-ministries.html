Ministries

Personal Ministries

Training and outreach that equip members to share their faith naturally.

Overview

Every member a witness

Personal Ministries helps members grow in confidence and skill so they can share the hope of Jesus in everyday conversations and intentional outreach. We offer practical training, small-group practice, and coordinated community events to make sharing faith simple and natural.

Training

Practical Courses & Workshops

  • Conversation Skills: Starting faith conversations, listening well, and sharing a brief testimony.
  • Bible Study Tools: How to use short Bible studies and evangelistic booklets conversationally.
  • Service-Led Outreach: Organising community service as an entry point to relationships.
  • Follow-up & Discipleship: Helping new friends connect with church life and spiritual growth.

Training is delivered as short workshops on Sabbath afternoons and weeknight small groups. Certificates and leader resources are available for volunteers.

Outreach

Community-Focused Outreach

We combine invitation-based and service-focused outreach: health screenings, community clean-ups, reading clubs, and family events that open doors for conversations about faith. Each outreach has clear roles for volunteers and simple follow-up paths to care and Bible study.

Health & Wellness

Short health talks and screenings, linking physical wellbeing with spiritual hope.

Family Events

Fairs, children’s programs, and fellowship meals that welcome neighbours.

One-to-One

Personal visits, prayer, and study with those who express interest.

Get Involved

Want to take part? Attend a training, join an outreach team, or sign up to host a follow-up group. Contact our Personal Ministries coordinator to learn about upcoming training dates and resources.
