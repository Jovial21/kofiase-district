Children's Ministry

Helping Children Love Jesus and His Word

Safe, joyful, age-appropriate ministry through Sabbath School, Bible stories, songs, prayer, memory verses, and creative learning.

Our Focus

Faith Foundations For Little Hearts

The Children's Ministry helps children know that Jesus loves them, understand Bible truth in simple ways, and feel at home in the church family.

Teachers and helpers use songs, stories, memory verses, prayer, crafts, activities, and guided worship moments to help children grow with joy and care.

View Children's Events

Programs

How Children's Ministry Serves

S

Sabbath School

Weekly Bible lessons, songs, prayer, and activities designed for children at their level of understanding.

B

Bible Stories

Engaging stories from Scripture that teach children about Jesus, obedience, kindness, faith, and courage.

M

Memory Verses

Simple Scripture memory activities that help children carry God's Word in their hearts.

W

Children's Worship

Opportunities for children to sing, pray, recite, and participate in worship programs.

C

Creative Lessons

Crafts, quizzes, drawing, drama, and practical activities that make Bible learning clear and memorable.

F

Family Support

Encouragement for parents and guardians to continue Bible learning, prayer, and worship at home.

Get Involved

Ways To Support Children

T

R

Resource Support

Help with lesson materials, stationery, Bible story aids, memory verse cards, and creative learning supplies.

P

Pray For Children

Pray for children, families, teachers, safety, spiritual growth, and the next generation of the church.
